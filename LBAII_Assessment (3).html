Leadership Styles
S1 — Directing
High Directive · Low Supportive
Specific instructions, close supervision. Best for people new to a task.
S2 — Coaching
High Directive · High Supportive
Direct and closely supervise while explaining decisions and soliciting input.
S3 — Supporting
Low Directive · High Supportive
Facilitate and support efforts, share decision-making. Best for competent people who need confidence.
S4 — Delegating
Low Directive · Low Supportive
Turn decisions over to the individual. Best for highly competent, committed performers.
